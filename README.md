# Amazon India Sales Dashboard | Product, Pricing & Category Performance Analysis

## Project Overview

Businesses operating in large e-commerce marketplaces generate vast amounts of product, pricing, review, and customer engagement data. Converting this information into actionable insights is essential for category management, pricing strategy, inventory planning, and business growth.

This project presents an executive sales dashboard built using Amazon India product data. The dashboard provides a consolidated view of product performance, pricing behavior, discount trends, customer ratings, and category-level insights to support data-driven decision-making.

The objective of this project is to transform raw product data into meaningful business intelligence that enables stakeholders to understand market performance and identify growth opportunities.

---

# Business Problem

E-commerce platforms manage thousands of products across multiple categories. Without effective reporting mechanisms, decision-makers face challenges in understanding:

* Which product categories drive marketplace activity
* How discounts influence product positioning
* Customer satisfaction levels across categories
* Pricing trends within the product catalog
* Product review distribution and engagement patterns

To address these challenges, a centralized dashboard was developed to analyze category performance, pricing structures, ratings, reviews, and discount behavior.

# Key Findings

### Electronics Dominates the Marketplace

Electronics represents the largest category in the dataset and contributes significantly to product availability and customer engagement.

### High Discount Activity

A substantial portion of products are positioned within medium-to-high discount ranges, indicating strong promotional activity.

### Ratings Remain Consistently Positive

Customer ratings are concentrated around favorable ranges, suggesting generally positive customer experiences.

### Price Segmentation Reveals Volume Opportunities

Lower and mid-priced product ranges contain a large share of listed products, highlighting the importance of value-focused market segments.

### Category Expansion Opportunities Exist

Some smaller categories demonstrate strong customer satisfaction despite limited product availability, suggesting potential growth opportunities.
